Floating Action Button :
       A button that is floated on the screen like something is floating on water.It is used for primary actions like to insert the
       new note in notes app or add a contact in contacts or networking app.

FAB in compose:
 Compose provides us a lot of variety of FABs
Compose gives us many types FABs to use individual according to the given condition
Types of FABs:
1.FloatingActionButton
2.ExtendedFloatingActionButton
3.SmallFloatingActionButton
4.SmallExtendedFloatingActionButton
5.MediumFloatingActionButton
6.MediumExtendedFloatingActionButton
7.LargeFloatingActionButton
8.LargeExtendedFloatingActionButton
